WHAT WE DO

At Mata Sundari Devi Charitable, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, holistic and quality healthcare to marginalized communities lacking access or means. Our expansive range of thoughtfully-designed facilities and services aim to foster wellbeing, preventive care, and treatment when needed. Charitable OPD: Ensuring accessibility to quality healthcare for those in need. General OPD: Comprehensive consultations for a wide spectrum of health concerns. Diagnostic Excellence: Cutting-edge facilities for All Blood Tests, Urine Tests, Digital X-Ray, Digital OPG, and ECG. Holistic Wellness: Experience rejuvenation with services like Hydra Facial, Ayurvedic Body Massage, and Steam Therapy. Specialized Care: Our team of skilled doctors cover various specialties, including General Medicine, Ayurveda, ENT, Dental, Dermatology, Gynecology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, Physiotherapy, Cardiovascular, and General Surgery. With a patient-first approach, we strive to deliver medical services with compassion and clinical expertise to foster healthier, more hopeful communities. Please reach out for any additional questions on utilizing our charitable healthcare facilities.

Mission

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.
