About Our Organization

Mata Sunder Devi is a Charitable Trust, Trust was Founded on 1st January 2024, where our noble mission is to extend unparalleled and affordable healthcare services to all, with a special focus on the underprivileged we take pride in offering a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet diverse medical
Our Services are - Charitable OPD , General OPD , Diagnostic Excellence , Urine Tests, Digital X-Ray, Digital OPG, and ECG. Holistic Wellness with services like Hydra Facial, Ayurvedic Body Massage, and Steam Therapy. Our team of skilled doctors cover various specialties including General Medicine, Ayurveda, ENT, Dental, Dermatology, Gynecology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, Physiotherapy, Cardiovascular, and General Surgery.
Why Choose Mata Sunder Devi Charitable Trust: Inclusive Care: We believe in healthcare for everyone, regardless of socio-economic backgrounds.Expert Physicians:Our clinic boasts a team of seasoned doctors, ensuring comprehensive healthcare solutions.State-of-the-Art Facilities:Equipped with the latest technology for accurate diagnostics and advanced treatments.Wellness Beyond Medicine: From therapeutic massages to advanced skincare, we promote holistic well-being.
Community Impact By choosing our services, you contribute to a noble cause, supporting our mission to serve the underprivileged. Step into a haven of health where compassionate care meets excellence at Mata Sunder Devi Charitable Trust.
